Every website needs a first post. This is mine.

Writing in public is something I've thought about for a while. The friction of starting always felt higher than the value of the output — until it didn't. So here we are.

## Why write?

There's a certain clarity that only comes from putting thoughts into words. Reading sharpens ideas; writing exposes the gaps. This space is mostly for me, but if something here is useful to you, that's a bonus.

## What to expect

No strict schedule, no theme. When something is worth writing about, I'll write about it. Expect a mix of:

- Things I'm learning or working on
- Ideas I keep returning to
- Occasionally, something more personal

Thanks for reading.
